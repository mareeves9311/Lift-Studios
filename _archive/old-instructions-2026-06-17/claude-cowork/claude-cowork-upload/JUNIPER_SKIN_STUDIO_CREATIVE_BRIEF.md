# Juniper Skin Studio Creative Brief

## Business Name

Juniper Skin Studio

## Category

Boutique skin studio / med spa

## Concept

A calm, modern skin studio for subtle, confidence-building treatments.

## Audience

Women ages 28-55 who want:

- Natural-looking results
- Clear treatment guidance
- A provider they trust
- A calm, non-intimidating booking experience
- Help choosing where to start

## Positioning Line

Subtle treatments. Clear guidance. Skin that still feels like you.

## Services

- Skin consultations
- Facials
- Skin treatments
- Injectable consultations
- Event prep
- Product recommendations

## Brand Personality

- Calm
- Trustworthy
- Soft but polished
- Expert but approachable
- Modern
- Subtle
- Guided
- Confidence-building

## Brand Palette

Soft Ivory:
#F8F4EF

Clay Rose:
#B98272

Deep Taupe:
#5D514B

Sage Gray:
#A9B2A4

Ink:
#1F1F1F

## Typography Direction

Headlines:
Playfair Display or another refined editorial serif.

Body:
Inter or another clean sans serif.

Labels:
Small uppercase sans serif with careful spacing.

## Logo Direction

Primary:
Minimal wordmark reading "Juniper Skin Studio."

Secondary:
JSS monogram or a simple line-based juniper leaf icon.

Logo should feel:
- Refined
- Calm
- Editorial
- Minimal
- Not overly spa-cliche

## Visual Direction

Use:
- Calm treatment room details
- Product shelf moments
- Provider consultation imagery
- Soft skin-care textures
- Neutral interior details
- Hands applying product
- Minimal product flatlays

Avoid:
- Overly clinical stock photos
- Overdone pink med spa aesthetic
- Generic beauty influencer visuals
- Unrealistic before/after claims
- Anything that feels too obviously AI-generated

## Homepage Direction

Hero headline:
Skin treatments that feel subtle, personal, and easy to understand.

Support copy:
A calm skin studio for guided consultations, natural-looking results, and treatment plans that fit your goals.

CTA buttons:
- Book a Consultation
- View Treatments

Homepage sections:
- Not Sure What To Book?
- Treatment Paths
- Provider Trust
- Client Love
- FAQs
- Book A Consultation

## Social Content Direction

Core pillars:

1. First-time trust
2. Treatment education
3. Provider expertise
4. Social proof
5. Event/seasonal prep

Example hooks:

- If you have been nervous to book your first skin treatment, start here.
- Not sure what to book? Here is where I would start based on your goal.
- 3 things to know before your first consultation.
- Botox vs. filler, explained simply.
- Your event skin prep timeline.

## Main Conversion Goal

Make unsure visitors feel safe enough to book a consultation.
