# Project Brief: MR Studio Content Growth Kit

## Owner

Megan Reeves

## Business Name

MR Studio

## Signature Offer

Content Growth Kit

## Positioning

MR Studio helps service-based and consumer-facing businesses turn their website and social media into a stronger first impression, with monthly content kits that make them easier to trust, book, visit, or buy from.

## Background

This project started as a website refresh MR for local businesses. It has expanded into a broader offer that includes:

- Website and social media audits
- Monthly UGC-style content buckets
- Social media content strategy
- Captions, hooks, and calendars
- Light branding and visual direction
- Homepage layout recommendations
- Offer/service positioning
- Optional website/brand refresh add-ons

The name should not be limited to "local" because the business may eventually expand to non-local service businesses, consumer brands, and e-commerce brands.

## Target Clients

Best early targets:

- Med spas
- Skin studios
- Estheticians
- Hair salons
- Nail salons
- Boutique fitness studios
- Cafes / brunch spots
- Interior designers
- Wedding photographers
- Local boutiques
- Cosmetic dentists

Why these work:

- They are visual.
- Trust matters before someone books.
- Their websites often do not match the quality of the actual service.
- They need consistent content but may not know what to post.
- A clear mini-audit can quickly demonstrate value.

## What Needs Improvement

The current Canva version is a good start but may still feel too template-like.

The next iteration should:

- Focus on one strong fictional business example.
- Make the pricing page look like a polished service menu.
- Show what the actual deliverables could look like.

- Show a realistic homepage layout.

- Make the offer feel tangible and premium.
