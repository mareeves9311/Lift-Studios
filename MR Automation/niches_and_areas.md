# Niches And Areas

## Best First Niches

These are strong because the website directly affects bookings, inquiries, or trust.

### Tier 1: Highest Priority

- Med spas
- Aesthetic clinics
- Estheticians
- Salons
- Day spas
- Wellness clinics
- Cosmetic dental offices
- Chiropractors
- Physical therapy clinics
- Boutique fitness studios
- Wedding photographers
- Wedding planners
- Interior designers

### Tier 2: Good Expansion Niches

- Local boutiques
- Restaurants and cafes with catering/private events
- Florists
- Home organizers
- Cleaning companies
- Landscapers
- Contractors with premium services
- Realtors and small real estate teams
- Therapists and coaches
- Pet groomers
- Dog trainers

### Avoid At First

- Very large corporate brands
- Businesses with no visible booking, inquiry, or purchase path
- Businesses with no budget indicators
- Businesses that only use Facebook and do not seem to value web presence
- Businesses with already excellent sites unless there is one obvious conversion issue

## Wide Outreach Strategy

Run searches by combining:

`[niche] + [city]`

Examples:

- med spa Harrisburg PA
- esthetician Hershey PA
- salon Camp Hill PA
- wedding photographer Lancaster PA
- interior designer Main Line PA
- cosmetic dentist Mechanicsburg PA

The goal is not to find every business. The goal is to find businesses with enough visible opportunity that a short, specific pitch feels useful.
