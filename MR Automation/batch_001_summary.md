## Batch Overview

Batch 001 includes 16 first-pass candidates across:

- Med spas
- Salons and day spas
- Cosmetic dentists
- Interior designers
- Estheticians
- Wedding photographers

## Highest Priority Angle

Start with med spas and aesthetics clinics.

Why:

- High client value
- Booking/consult path matters
- Easy to explain ROI
- Aesthetic businesses care about presentation
- Many have strong services but generic or crowded messaging

## First Outreach Test

Use two message styles.

### Version A: Soft Audit Offer

Ask if they want 3 quick recommendations.

Best for:

- Scores 13-16
- Businesses where we have a specific issue but no contact name

### Version B: More Direct Paid Offer

Pitch a homepage refresh at $650.

Best for:

- Strongest candidates after manual review
- Businesses with obvious CTA/mobile/service-page issues

## Manual Review Checklist Before Sending

Open each website and confirm:

- Is the business active?
- Is there a booking/contact path?
- Does the site look weaker than their Instagram/reviews?
- Can we name one issue honestly?
- Is the business likely to afford $500-$1,500?
