# MR Studio Service Menu

## What We Do

Most service businesses don't have a content problem. They have a capacity problem. There's no budget for an in-house team, no time to make it a priority, and so the website and social presence quietly fall behind the quality of the actual work.

MR Studio closes that gap. We handle the strategy, creative direction, and production so your business makes a stronger first impression at every touchpoint — and gives you the system to keep showing up consistently.

We work in three areas:

**Brand Identity** — logo, color palette, typography, brand voice, and a brand book you can hand to anyone who touches your brand.

**Content Direction** — strategy, scripts, concepts, hooks, and calendars. You get everything you need to film and post yourself.

**Content Delivery** — finished, ready-to-post graphics, carousels, and designed posts built from your image bank. You upload. We handle the rest.
