# Workflow

## Goal

Create a repeatable system for finding good-fit businesses, auditing their websites quickly, and sending useful outreach at scale.

## Daily Workflow

### 1. Build A Lead Batch

Search one niche and one area at a time.

Examples:

- med spa Harrisburg PA
- salon Hershey PA
- esthetician Camp Hill PA
- wedding photographer Lancaster PA
- interior designer Main Line PA

Add 25-50 businesses to `lead_tracker.csv`.

### 2. Score Each Lead

Use `candidate_scoring_rubric.md`.

Only outreach:

- Score 13+: personalized email
- Score 9-12: wide outreach email
- Score under 9: skip

### 3. Identify One Specific Issue

The outreach only works if it feels real.

Find one visible issue:

- Weak homepage headline
- Booking button hard to find
- Services are confusing
- Mobile layout feels dated
- No testimonials near CTA
- Instagram looks better than website
- Contact form is buried
- Too much generic copy

### 4. Send Outreach

Use the templates in `outreach_templates.md`.

For the strongest leads, offer to send 3 quick recommendations.

### 5. Follow Up

Follow-up schedule:

- Day 3: Follow-up 1
- Day 7: Follow-up 2
- Day 21: recycle if relevant

### 6. If They Reply

Send a short mini-audit.

Mini-audit format:

- What is working
- Issue 1
- Issue 2
- Issue 3
- Recommended first fix
- Paid offer

## Weekly Volume Target

Start realistic:

- 100 new leads per week
- 60 scored leads
- 30-40 outreach emails
- 10 follow-ups
- Goal: 1-3 interested replies

Once messaging works:

- 250-500 leads per week
- 100-200 outreach emails
- 2-5 booked calls or paid audits

## First Automation To Build

Lead intake and scoring helper:

Input:

- Business name
- Website
- Category
- City

Output:

- Initial score
- Top website issue
- Suggested pitch angle
- Draft outreach email
