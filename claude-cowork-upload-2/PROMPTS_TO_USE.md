## Prompt: Canva V2 Generation

Create a polished 10-page client-facing report for MR Studio titled "Content Growth Kit." This is an outreach attachment for service-based and consumer-facing businesses. It should feel premium, warm, modern, boutique studio, not corporate. Do not mention AI.

Core positioning:
MR Studio helps businesses turn their website and social media into a stronger first impression, with monthly content kits that make them easier to trust, book, visit, or buy from.

Use one fictional client example only:
Juniper Skin Studio, a boutique skin studio / med spa.

Audience:
Women 28-55 who want natural-looking results, clear guidance, and a trusted provider.

Positioning line:
Subtle treatments. Clear guidance. Skin that still feels like you.

Page plan:

1. Cover: MR Studio: Content Growth Kit. Subtitle: Website, social, and content systems that make your business easier to trust, book, visit, or buy from.

4. Example Client Concept: Juniper Skin Studio, concept, audience, conversion goal.
5. Brand Direction: Juniper Skin Studio wordmark, JSS monogram or leaf mark, palette, fonts, visual direction.
6. Homepage First Impression: realistic homepage mockup/wireframe with hero, CTAs, sections.
7. Social Content Examples: mock Instagram/Reel cards with hooks.
8. Monthly Content Bucket: week-by-week plan and deliverables.
9. What You Walk Away With: outcomes.
10. Next Step: review mini-audit, choose package, approve Starter Content Kit or request walkthrough.

Editorial med spa aesthetic, neutral palette, brand swatches, social mockups, homepage frame, clean service pricing menu. Avoid clutter and generic corporate graphics.
