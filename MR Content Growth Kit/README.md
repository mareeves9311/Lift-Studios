## Core Offer

**Local Content Growth Kit** helps local businesses look more credible online and post consistently without having to become content creators themselves.

Services:
- Website audit and quick-win recommendations
- Instagram/TikTok/social presence audit
- Monthly UGC-style content bucket
- Captions, hooks, and content calendar
- Light branding and positioning help
- Optional content refreshes for offers, launches, and seasonal moments

## Recommended First Move

Pick one business and create a free mini-audit:

1. Review their website, Instagram, TikTok, Google profile, and booking flow.
2. Identify 5 quick wins.
3. Create 6 sample content ideas for them.
4. Package it as a short lookbook.
5. Send a warm, specific outreach message.
