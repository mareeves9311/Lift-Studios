What is working:
- Clear breakfast/brunch/lunch occasion
- Strong brand name and "Fuel Your Morning" concept
- Specialty Guatemalan coffee differentiator
- Menu items with strong visual/social potential
- Online ordering already available

Make the Mechanicsburg location easier to understand, easier to choose, and easier to order from in the first 10 seconds.

Morning Fuel has a strong foundation. The next layer is turning the menu, coffee, ambiance, and local location into a consistent content system that drives first-time visits and repeat orders.
