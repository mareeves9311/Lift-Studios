# Tool Stack

## Strategy + Copy

ChatGPT / Claude:
- Website audit
- Social audit
- Captions
- Hooks
- UGC scripts
- Content calendar
- Brand positioning

## Static Visuals

Playground:
- Branded static content
- Lifestyle/product concepts
- Moodboards
- Ad-style visuals
- Creative direction samples

Canva:
- Lookbooks
- Client PDFs
- Carousel posts
- Content calendars
- Brand templates

Adobe Express:
- Fast social templates
- AI-assisted design
- Quick resize/export workflows

## Video

Runway:
- Higher-end AI video concepts
- Lifestyle visuals
- Product/service atmosphere
- Short-form creative tests

OpusClip:
- Turn raw client footage into clips
- Captions
- Reframing
- B-roll
- Scheduling
- Brand templates

Predis.ai:
- Social posts
- UGC videos
- E-commerce/product videos
- Content calendar and scheduling

## Workflow

1. Audit business manually.
2. Generate content strategy.
3. Create sample lookbook in Canva or Google Slides.
4. Build sample visuals in Playground/Canva.
5. Create UGC scripts with ChatGPT/Claude.
6. Use Runway/OpusClip if video samples are needed.
7. Package everything as a monthly content bucket.

## Ethical Positioning

Do not lead with "AI made this." Lead with the result:

"Done-for-you content strategy and monthly social assets."

If asked, say:

"I use AI-assisted production tools to keep the process efficient, but the strategy, editing, taste, and final direction are mine."
