## Page 7: Social Content Examples

### Reel 1: Nervous To Book

Hook:
If you have been nervous to book your first skin treatment, start here.

Visual notes:
Exterior, calm entry, consultation moment, provider explaining options, treatment room detail.

Caption direction:
Normalize not knowing what to book first. Position the consultation as the easiest starting point.

CTA:
Book a consultation if you are not sure where to start.

### Reel 2: Treatment Matchmaker

Hook:
Not sure what to book? Here is where I would start based on your goal.

Visual notes:
Goal cards: texture, fine lines, dullness, event prep.

Caption direction:
Match common goals to first-step appointment types without overpromising.

CTA:
Save this before your first appointment.

### Carousel: Before Your First Consultation

Hook:
3 things to know before your first skin consultation.

Slides:
1. You do not need to know exactly what to book.
2. A good provider should explain your options clearly.
3. Subtle, realistic results are often the goal.

CTA:
Send this to someone thinking about booking.

### Carousel: Botox vs. Filler

Hook:
Botox vs. filler, explained simply.

Slides:
1. Botox helps movement lines.
2. Filler supports volume and contour.
3. A consultation helps decide what is appropriate.
4. Natural-looking results start with the right plan.

CTA:
Book a consultation for guidance.

### Static Post: Event Skin Prep

Hook:
Your event skin prep timeline.

Visual notes:
Minimal timeline graphic with 12 weeks, 8 weeks, 4 weeks, 1 week.

CTA:
Save this before wedding, vacation, or event season.
