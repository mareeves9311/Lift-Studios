# Local Content Growth Kit - Outreach HQ

Use this Drive folder as the operating home for the MR.

## Goal

Create a strong first impression with local businesses by sending:

1. A specific mini-audit for their business.
2. A polished overview attachment that shows what the service can produce.
3. A clear, low-pressure outreach message.
4. A simple next step: approve a Starter Content Kit or request a short walkthrough.

## What We Are Offering

Local Content Growth Kit helps local businesses look more credible online and post consistently without having to become full-time content creators.

Core services:
- Website first-impression audit
- Social media profile/content audit
- Monthly UGC-style content bucket
- Brand/content direction
- Website homepage and service-page recommendations
- Captions, hooks, content calendar, and posting direction

## Who We Are Going To First

Best first targets:
- Cafes and brunch spots
- Med spas and estheticians
- Hair salons
- Nail salons
- Boutique fitness studios
- Interior designers
- Wedding photographers
- Local boutiques
- Cosmetic dentists

Why these work:
- They are visual.
- They need trust before someone books or visits.
- They usually have a strong service or menu but weak content systems.
- They can understand the value quickly when shown examples.

## Before Sending Any Outreach

For each business:

1. Confirm the website URL and contact path.
2. Review Instagram manually.
3. Pull 3-5 specific observations.
4. Name the most obvious website or content opportunity.
5. Add them to the tracker.
6. Create a mini-audit.
7. Send a short, specific DM or email.
8. Attach or link the reusable overview.
9. Offer to send the mini-audit or do a short walkthrough.

## Strong First Outreach Formula

Subject or opening:
Quick content ideas for [Business Name]

Message:
I noticed [specific compliment]. I also saw an opportunity to [specific quick win]. I put together a short mini-audit with ideas for [business-specific outcome]. Happy to send it over if helpful.

Attachment:
Local Content Growth Kit - Service Overview + Sample Content Examples

Follow-up:
No pressure at all, but I made this specifically for your business and thought it might be useful.
