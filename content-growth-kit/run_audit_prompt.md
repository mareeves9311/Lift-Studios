# Lift Studio: Run Audit Command

## How to Use

Say: **"Run audit for [Business Name] — [website URL] — [Instagram handle]"**

Claude Code will:
1. Research the business website, social presence, reviews, and competitors
2. Diagnose website, social, brand, and content opportunity
3. Write a prospect-specific mini-audit
4. Recommend the best-fit Lift offer
5. Identify whether the prospect needs a written audit only, a lookbook, or a custom proof-of-work showcase
6. Generate a Claude Design brief only when a visual mockup would help sell the opportunity

---

## The Audit Areas

Every audit evaluates the same eight areas. Not every area needs equal weight; pick the ones where the gap between current state and potential is clearest.

**1. Website First Impression**
Can a new visitor understand what they do, who it is for, why it matters, and what to do next?

**2. Conversion Path**
Is the booking, inquiry, order, visit, or shop path easy to follow?

**3. Social First Impression**
Do the bio, pinned posts, highlights, content cadence, and visuals make the business feel active and trustworthy?

**4. Brand Clarity**
Does the voice, visual direction, audience, and positioning feel cohesive across website and social?

**5. Content Pillars**
What recurring series can be built from their actual offers, FAQs, reviews, products, services, team, process, and differentiators?

**6. Trust Proof**
Are reviews, credentials, founder story, team expertise, results, FAQs, process, and policies easy to find before someone decides?

**7. Visual / UGC Potential**
Are there natural short-form or static content moments that could become monthly content?

**8. Offer Fit**
Which Lift service is the smallest logical next step?

---

## Exhibit Selection Logic

After the audit, pick the 2-3 areas where the gap between current state and potential is most dramatic. These become the exhibit pages only if a visual showcase is worth building.

| If the business has... | Use this exhibit |
|---|---|
| Poor or no food/product photography | Composition + Crop or Light + Color |
| Generic or missing captions | Hook + Caption |
| Weak or inconsistent branding | Branding + Type |
| No clear first impression on website | Homepage Hero Concept |
| No content system at all | Monthly Content Bucket Card |

For content-first leads, a monthly content bucket can be more persuasive than a website mockup.
For brand-first leads, always include at least one brand direction concept.
For website-first leads, lead with the homepage/conversion path and use content as the follow-through.
