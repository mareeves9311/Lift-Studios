# First 30-Day Launch Plan

## Week 1: Build The Example

Pick one niche:
- Medspa
- Salon
- Boutique fitness
- Cafe

Create:
- One mini-audit
- One lookbook
- 6 sample content ideas
- 3 UGC-style scripts
- One outreach message

Goal: Have one polished sample you can reuse.

## Week 2: Build A Target List

Find 20 local businesses.

Prioritize businesses that:
- Have a decent service/product but weak content
- Post inconsistently
- Have a dated website
- Have no clear pinned posts
- Have good reviews but poor social storytelling
- Are owner-operated or small-team

Track:
- Business name
- Website
- Instagram
- Contact email
- Owner/manager name
- One specific compliment
- One obvious opportunity

## Week 3: Send 10 Mini-Audit Offers

Send:
- 5 Instagram DMs
- 5 emails

Offer a free mini-audit, not a paid package.

Goal:
Book 2 conversations or get permission to send 2 mini-audits.

## Week 4: Convert One Client

After sending the mini-audit, pitch:

"I can turn this into a monthly content system so you are not starting from scratch every time you need to post."

Offer:
- Starter Content Kit: $500-$750/month
- Audit-only option: $150-$300

Goal:
Sell one starter month.

## Minimum Viable Deliverable For First Client

Do not overbuild.

Send:
- 1 short website/social audit
- 4 Reel concepts/scripts
- 8 post/carousel ideas
- 12 captions/hooks
- 1 monthly calendar

Ask for feedback after two weeks.
