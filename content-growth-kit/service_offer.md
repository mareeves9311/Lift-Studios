# Service Offer

## Positioning

I help businesses turn their website and social presence into a clearer, more polished first impression, then give them the content system to keep showing up consistently.

## Client-Facing Offer

### Website + Social Audit

A concise audit of your current online presence, focused on what a potential customer sees before deciding whether to book, visit, or buy.

Includes:
- Website first impression review
- Mobile flow and booking path review
- Instagram/TikTok/social profile review
- Bio, highlights, pinned posts, and visual consistency review
- Quick-win recommendations
- Content opportunities based on your business goals

### Monthly UGC-Style Content Bucket

A monthly batch of ready-to-use content designed to make your business feel active, trustworthy, and easy to understand.

Includes:
- Short-form video concepts
- UGC-style scripts
- Static post and carousel concepts
- Captions and hooks
- Monthly content calendar
- Seasonal/local content ideas
- Optional posting guidance

### Brand + Content Direction

Light brand support for businesses that need a clearer voice or more consistent look.

Includes:
- Brand vibe direction
- Content pillars
- Offer positioning
- Visual style notes
- Bio rewrite
- Highlight/pinned post recommendations

## What Makes This Valuable

Most local businesses do not need a huge agency. They need:
- A clearer first impression
- Better content ideas
- Consistency
- More trust signals
- A reason for customers to book now

This service gives them the marketing brain, content plan, and creative assets without making them figure it out themselves.

Your business already has the service/product. I help make your online presence match the quality of what you offer.
