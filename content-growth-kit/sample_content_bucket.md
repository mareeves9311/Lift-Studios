# Sample Monthly Content Bucket

This is an example of what a client receives each month.

## Starter Monthly Bucket

Best for businesses that need consistency and a stronger online presence.

Deliverables:
- 4 short-form video concepts
- 4 UGC-style scripts
- 8 static post/carousel concepts
- 12 captions
- 12 hooks
- 1 monthly content calendar
- 1 short audit note with next-month recommendations

## Growth Monthly Bucket

Best for businesses that want a stronger push around booking, launches, or local awareness.

Deliverables:
- 8 short-form video concepts
- 8 UGC-style scripts
- 12 static post/carousel concepts
- 20 captions
- 20 hooks
- 1 monthly content calendar
- 1 campaign concept
- 1 monthly website/social optimization note

## Content Pillars

### Education

Goal: Help customers understand the service/product and reduce hesitation.

Examples:
- "3 things to know before booking [service]"
- "What most people get wrong about [topic]"
- "How to choose the right [service/product]"

### Social Proof

Goal: Build trust.

Examples:
- Testimonial graphics
- Before/after stories
- Customer POV content
- "Why clients come back"

### Service / Product Spotlight

Goal: Explain offers clearly.

Examples:
- "Is [service] right for you?"
- "What happens during your first visit"
- "Our most booked service this month"

### Behind The Scenes

Goal: Make the business feel human.

Examples:
- Day in the life
- Meet the owner/team
- Product prep
- Studio/shop walk-through

### Local / Seasonal

Goal: Make content timely.

Examples:
- Local event tie-ins
- Holiday promos
- Seasonal services
- Weather/lifestyle hooks

## Example UGC-Style Video Formula

Hook:
"I found the easiest way to [desired outcome] in [city]."

Body:
Show the business, service/product, and customer benefit.

Proof:
Mention reviews, results, experience, or repeat customers.

CTA:
"Book online," "Stop in this week," or "DM them for availability."
