# Morning Fuel Audit To Design Workflow

## Recommendation

Use a two-step flow:

1. **Audit / diagnosis**
   - Best tool: Claude.ai with web search enabled, or another browser-enabled research tool that can actually view Instagram and Google Business Profile.
   - Reason: Instagram and Google Business Profile are the most important missing surfaces for a real Morning Fuel audit. This environment can access the Morning Fuel website, but Instagram is blocked from direct fetch here.

2. **Creative brief / mockup production**
   - Best tool: Codex for structuring the audit into a clean design brief, Drive/Canva organization, and file updates.
   - Best design tool: Canva for the first prospect-facing attachment; Figma later if the website homepage mockup needs to look more realistic.
   - Best video tool: skip for first send; use UGC scripts/storyboards first. Use CapCut, Creatify, or Predis later if they ask for examples.

## Why This Flow Works

The diagnosis needs live business context:
- Instagram bio
- Recent posts
- Reels
- Highlights
- Pinned posts
- Engagement signals
- Google reviews
- Google Business Profile photos
- Website and ordering path

The design brief needs structured strategy:
- What is working
- What is costing them
- What to fix first
- What to mock up
- What the content examples should show
- What offer to pitch

Keeping those separate prevents the design from becoming generic.
