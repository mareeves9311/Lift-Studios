**Overall design direction:**
Warm, boutique-feminine energy — not clinical, not generic. Think editorial jewelry brand meets local shop warmth. Use gold and cream tones to tie the two exhibit pages together. Clean numbered sections, generous whitespace, high-contrast before/after panels. The before panels should feel noticeably flat; the after panels should feel alive.
