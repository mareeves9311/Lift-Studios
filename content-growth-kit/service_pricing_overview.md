# Service + Pricing Overview

## Positioning

Lift Studio helps service-based and consumer-facing businesses turn their website and social media into a clearer, more polished first impression.

The focus is:
- Website and social audits
- Brand/content direction
- Homepage copy and layout recommendations
- Ready-to-film UGC-style video concepts
- Social post and carousel concepts
- Captions, hooks, and content calendars
- Content systems that help businesses show up consistently
