# Pricing

These are starter prices. Raise them once there are 2-3 examples or a first paid client.

## Core Packages

### Mini-Audit Starter

Price: $250-$350 one-time

Includes:
- Website first-impression review
- Homepage clarity review
- Booking, order, or inquiry path review
- Instagram/social first-impression notes
- 5-7 quick wins
- Content opportunities
- Recommended next step

Best for:
Businesses that want a clear starting point before committing monthly.

### Starter Content Kit

Price: $650/month

Includes:
- 6 ready-to-film UGC-style video concepts
- 8 static post or carousel concepts
- 15 caption hooks
- 1 monthly content calendar
- Bio and pinned post recommendations
- Light website/social quick-win notes

Best for:
Businesses that need consistent monthly content direction without a full agency retainer.

### Content Bank

Price: $950/month

Includes:
- 10 ready-to-film UGC-style video concepts
- 10 static post or carousel concepts
- 25 caption hooks
- 1 monthly content calendar
- 3 pinned post recommendations
- 1 monthly promo, offer, or campaign idea
- Light brand/content direction notes

Best for:
Businesses that want a fuller monthly bank of content ideas across Reels, posts, carousels, and captions.

### Growth Content Kit

Price: $1,350/month

Includes:
- 12-15 ready-to-film UGC-style video concepts
- 12-16 static post or carousel concepts
- 30 caption hooks
- 1 monthly content calendar
- Offer/content direction
- Light brand direction
- Website/social quick-win notes
- Bio, highlight, and pinned post recommendations
- 1 campaign, seasonal, or promotion concept

Best for:
Businesses that want stronger strategy, deeper content planning, and clearer conversion direction.
